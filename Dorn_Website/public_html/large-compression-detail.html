My Awesome Product

This pipe trunk is a larger compression fit model. This model allows you to arrange different size pipes in whatever way you would like. When the lid is closed it creates individual pockets for each pipe, tin, or accessory. If you need to rearrange the pipes or accessories, take them out and allow the foam to reset. Then start over. Size is: 11 in. x 15 in. x 6 in. It comes in four colors: black, red, green, and purple. Made in the USA! Pipes not included.
